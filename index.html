Napco VizBuilts 360

This viewer uses YouTube as the video source, but the look-around controls are provided outside the YouTube viewing window. This avoids relying on YouTube's embedded drag/touch 360 controls.

QR URL format:

https://vizbuilts.github.io/vr360/?video=VIDEO_ID&t=2.7&yaw=176&pitch=0&roll=0&fov=80

  • Arrow buttons adjust yaw and pitch.
  • Roll buttons adjust camera tilt.
  • Zoom buttons adjust FOV.
  • Keyboard: arrow keys look around, plus/minus zoom, space play/pause, R reapplies QR view.

Internet is required for YouTube playback. The PWA shell can install on a device, but the YouTube video still streams from YouTube.
